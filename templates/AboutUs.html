Decvin Labs was founded in 1992 by a team of young entrepreneurs who believed in improving the quality of life of poultry, thereby improving the quality of life of farmers. We initiated the company to provide to the need of the farmers and the veterinary doctors.

There was tremendous necessity for providing quality feed supplements mainly to the poultry farmers while being cost effective. Poor quality of feed supplements resulted in problems in the poultry e.g. reduced egg production, leg weakness, poor overall health of the bird etc. The farmer was looking for solutions for the problems. There were many manufacturers in the market who did not understand the necessity of the quality in feed supplement requirements. We started the company with a hope to be an answer to the situation.

We are recognized as an organization that stands for quality and values. We take pride in gaining confidence and retaining our customers. As a result of continued perseverance and dedication, we have always been recognized for the consistency of quality and trust. We work closely with our customers while keeping their best interest in our minds always, thus we have been able to retain almost all of our customers since inception

Our future plans are to expand our territory and to foray into Aquaculture and special therapeutic categories for other companion animals.Our aim is to become the partner of choice for veterinarians, feed manufacturers and animal caregivers.
